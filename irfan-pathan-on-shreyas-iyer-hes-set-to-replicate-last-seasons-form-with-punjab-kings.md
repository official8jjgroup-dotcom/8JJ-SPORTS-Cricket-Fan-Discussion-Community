# Irfan Pathan on Shreyas Iyer: “He’s Set to Replicate Last Season’s Form with Punjab Kings”

Former India all‑rounder **Irfan Pathan** has expressed confidence that **Shreyas Iyer** can replicate his remarkable 2025 **Indian Premier League (IPL)** campaign while leading the **Punjab Kings** in the 2026 season. Iyer’s impressive performance last year — finishing as the team’s top scorer with **604 runs in 17 innings**, including six fifties at a strike rate above 175 — set high expectations for the upcoming tournament. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

Pathan, speaking on JioHotstar’s *Game Plan – Know Your Team*, described Iyer as a **proven performer** whose experience, maturity, and ability to handle pressure make him well‑placed to build on past success. According to Pathan, Iyer doesn’t need IPL runs to force his way back into India’s T20I squad, underscoring his established status in both domestic and franchise cricket. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

## Leadership and Tactical Acumen

Pathan highlighted Iyer’s leadership partnership with head coach **Ricky Ponting**, noting their combined tactical insight and motivational skills have been key to Punjab Kings’ balance. Under their guidance, PBKS emerged as a competitive side with a blend of experienced internationals and promising local talent. Pathan pointed to Iyer’s calm approach in high‑pressure situations, such as his match‑winning 87* in crucial encounters, as evidence of his growth as both a captain and a batter. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

## Strong Batting Lineup

Alongside Iyer, the Punjab Kings boast a formidable top order. **Prabhsimran Singh** and **Priyansh Arya** delivered significant contributions last season, providing stability and firepower at the top of the order. Pathan explained that this batting depth allows Iyer to pace his innings strategically while taking advantage of scoring opportunities throughout the match. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}

The middle order also features a mix of Indian talent and overseas finishers such as **Marcus Stoinis** and **Mitch Owen**, adding further scoring potential in the latter overs. According to Pathan, this balance between established and emerging players enhances the team’s ability to post or chase competitive totals. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}

## A Tactical Bowling Attack

Beyond batting, Iyer will rely on a pace attack led by **Arshdeep Singh** and **Marco Jansen**, supported by other seam options like **Azmatullah Omarzai** and **Xavier Bartlett**. Pathan praised the bowling unit’s depth and versatility, highlighting how effective rotation of bowlers and smart field placements can make a difference in tight games. He also pointed out that Iyer’s tactical awareness with the ball complements his batting leadership. :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}

## Looking Ahead to IPL 2026

As Punjab Kings prepare to begin their IPL 2026 campaign against Gujarat Titans on March 31, Shreyas Iyer enters the season with both expectation and confidence from pundits like Pathan. If Iyer can replicate his previous form and lead PBKS to another strong showing, he could further solidify his role as one of India’s premier T20 talents and a candidate for continued national selection. :contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}
