# SRH Predicted Playing XI for IPL 2026: Strong Core Built for Title Push

Sunrisers Hyderabad head into IPL 2026 with a well-balanced and aggressive squad built around explosive top-order batting and versatile all-round options. With a core group of match-winners retained and reinforced through the auction, SRH look set to continue their high-intensity brand of cricket.

The expected playing XI is likely to revolve around Travis Head and Abhishek Sharma at the top, followed by Ishan Kishan in the middle order providing stability and wicketkeeping duties. The presence of Heinrich Klaasen and Nitish Kumar Reddy adds firepower and flexibility, while Liam Livingstone strengthens the all-round department with both bat and ball.

In the bowling unit, Pat Cummins leads the attack alongside Harshal Patel and Jaydev Unadkat, offering experience and control in different phases of the innings. Young leg-spinner Zeeshan Ansari is expected to play a key role in the middle overs, with additional support from Harsh Dubey and other emerging options depending on conditions.

Overall, SRH’s 2026 setup reflects a clear intent to dominate through aggressive batting depth and adaptable bowling combinations, making them strong contenders for a deep playoff run.
