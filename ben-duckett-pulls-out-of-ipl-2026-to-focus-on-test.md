# [Ben Duckett Pulls Out of IPL 2026 to Focus on Test Cricket](https://8jjsports.com/ben-duckett-pulls-out-of-ipl-2026-to-focus-on-test/)

England batter **Ben Duckett** has decided to pull out of the **IPL 2026** season in order to focus on his **Test cricket career**, prioritising long-term international goals over franchise commitments.

Duckett, who had been set to feature in the tournament for the first time, informed his IPL franchise of his decision ahead of the season. The move reflects his intent to regain form and consistency in the longer format following a challenging period in international cricket.

The left-handed opener has been an important part of England’s red-ball setup, and with key Test series ahead, he has chosen to dedicate time to preparation, training, and performance improvement.

His withdrawal highlights the increasing challenge players face in balancing packed international schedules with high-profile T20 leagues like the IPL.

For his franchise, Duckett’s absence means a reshuffle at the top of the batting order, with management now expected to explore replacement options before the tournament begins.
