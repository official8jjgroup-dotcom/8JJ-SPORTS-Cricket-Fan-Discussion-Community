# [PSL Ball-Tampering: 5 Key Facts on Fakhar Zaman Case](https://8jj.com/psl-ball-tampering-5-key-facts-on-fakhar-zaman-case/)

The Pakistan Super League (PSL) saw a **controversial ball-tampering incident** involving **Fakhar Zaman**, which has captured widespread attention. Here are the **five key facts** surrounding the case:

### 1. Incident Occurred During a Crucial PSL Match  
During a high-stakes game between **Lahore Qalandars** and **Karachi Kings**, umpire intervention occurred when Karachi required 14 runs in the final over. Lahore players were seen handling and discussing the ball, triggering suspicion of tampering.

### 2. Penalty Affected Match Outcome  
Umpires awarded **five penalty runs** to Karachi and replaced the ball. These runs proved decisive as Karachi chased down the target and won by four wickets.

### 3. Fakhar Zaman and Teammates Charged  
The **Pakistan Cricket Board (PCB)** officially charged Fakhar Zaman with ball-tampering. **Shaheen Afridi** and **Haris Rauf** were also involved in the incident, though the primary charge is against Zaman.

### 4. Disciplinary Proceedings Underway  
Zaman denied the charge during the initial hearing. A **second hearing within 48 hours** will determine guilt and potential sanctions, including match bans.

### 5. Strong Reactions From the Cricket Community  
The incident has sparked criticism from former players, analysts, and fans. Debates on sportsmanship, ethics, and fair play have dominated online discussions and commentary.
