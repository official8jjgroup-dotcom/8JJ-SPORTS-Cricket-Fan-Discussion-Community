# [Dhoni Honors 3 World Cup Winners at CSK Event Ahead of IPL 2026](https://8jjsports.com/dhoni-honors-3-world-cup-winners-in-csk-event/)

Chennai Super Kings legend **MS Dhoni** paid tribute to three of India’s recent World Cup heroes during a special pre-season event ahead of IPL 2026.

At the fan event held in Chennai, Dhoni honoured **Sanju Samson**, **Shivam Dube**, and young captain **Ayush Mhatre**, recognising their contributions to India’s recent global triumphs. The ceremony celebrated both the senior team’s ICC Men’s T20 World Cup 2026 victory and India’s Under-19 World Cup success. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

Dhoni presented special mementoes to the trio, highlighting their performances on the world stage. Samson played a key role in India’s T20 World Cup campaign with match-winning knocks, while Dube contributed with both bat and ball during the tournament. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

Meanwhile, Ayush Mhatre captained India to victory in the Under-19 World Cup, further strengthening the country’s dominance in global cricket competitions. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

The event, organised by Chennai Super Kings as part of their IPL 2026 preparations, also brought together players, fans, and team officials, building momentum ahead of the new season.

CSK, led by a refreshed squad and inspired by recent international success, will be aiming to bounce back strongly in IPL 2026 after a disappointing previous campaign.

Dhoni’s gesture not only celebrated achievement but also reinforced the connection between international success and franchise cricket, motivating players as they head into another competitive IPL season.
